Drop Shipping

Drop shipping is a business model that allows entrepreneurs to start an online store without holding inventory. Instead, you list products from suppliers on your website. When a customer orders, the supplier ships the product directly to them.

This model is great for beginners with low capital. You focus on marketing and branding while suppliers handle fulfillment. Use platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, or BigCommerce to build your store and connect with global suppliers.
