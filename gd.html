Graphic Design

Graphic design is the art of visual communication using typography, imagery, color, and layout. Designers create logos, branding, social media content, websites, and marketing materials that captivate and convert audiences.

With tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Canva, and Figma, anyone can start learning graphic design. Whether for personal brands, businesses, or freelance projects, design is a vital skill in the digital age.
